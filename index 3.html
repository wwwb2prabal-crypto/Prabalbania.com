PRABAL BANIA

A Music Producer • Flutist • Composer
Prabal Bania is an Indian music producer and professional flutist dedicated to the intersection of traditional melody and modern mindfulness. With a deep understanding of Raga therapy and contemporary production, Prabal creates immersive sonic environments designed to reduce anxiety, promote emotional healing, and foster deep relaxation.

The Divine Breath : A Journey to Inner Peace

• The flute is the oldest instrument known to humanity, but in the hands of a seeker, it is something much more— it is the Voice of the Infinite. Just as hollow bamboo must surrender itself to become a vessel for the wind, the human mind finds peace when it surrenders to the celestial notes of the flute.
• The flute is the Instrument of the Gods. It represents the “Anahata” (the unstruck sound)— the cosmic vibration that existed before the world began.
